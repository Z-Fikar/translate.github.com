Our Products

Software solutions for localization

Pootle

Oneline translation platform. Pootle allows your professional or community translators to easily complete localization tasks. For you it becomes easier to manage and monitor the distributed work.

Virtaal

Desktop translation tool. For many minority languages online translation is not an option due to connectivity costs and reliability. Virtaal provides a powerful yet simple interface that follows many of the layout choices of Pootle making it an easy tool to use.

Translate Toolkit

Localization engineering toolkit. The Translate Toolkit provides tools to process, transform and manage localization files.

Amagama

Translation Memory server. Amagama stores all your previous translations and provides translation suggestions for both Pootle and Virtaal. Deploying your own Amagama server allows you to host your own data internally or privately.
